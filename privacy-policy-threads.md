# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 8 Jul 2026_

Threads_App (“we”, “our”, “us”) only stores data needed to operate our Threads automations.  
We **do not sell or share** personal information with third parties.

| Data | Why we keep it | Retention |
|------|----------------|-----------|
| Thread ID | Identify which account to publish to | Until you ask us to delete |
| Access token (server-side) | Authenticate posts | Rotated every 90 days |
